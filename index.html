About me

I am a PhD student in Economics at Insper, supervised by Prof. Hedibert Lopes.

My research lies at the intersection of economics and statistics, with a focus on Bayesian nonparametrics, applied machine learning, and econometrics. My work combines methodological development with applications to finance and macroeconomics.
